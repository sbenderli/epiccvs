About

Epic CVs is a playful project that imagines how history’s most famous figures might present themselves in today’s professional world. From conquerors to poets, emperors to inventors, their résumés are reimagined with a mix of humor, history, and modern career-speak.

This site is purely for fun and laughs and is best viewed as historical satire.

I shamelessly use AI tools for every part of this site. I do my best to keep things historically accurate, but make no promises.

This isn’t a business. If ad revenue ever does come in, it will be donated to charity.
